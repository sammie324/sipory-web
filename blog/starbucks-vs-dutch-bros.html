Starbucks vs Dutch Bros: Price, Menu, Caffeine, and Vibe Compared

March 202610 min readHead-to-Head · 8 Categories · Energy Wars

Starbucks has 16,000+ US locations and 90+ drinks. Dutch Bros has ~1,136 locations across 25 states — but it's the fastest-growing coffee chain in America, hitting $1.64 billion in revenue in 2025 (up 27.9% YoY) and outperforming Starbucks on same-store sales. These chains serve fundamentally different experiences, and knowing when each wins saves you money and gets you a better drink.

The Experience Gap

Starbucks = "third place" café with seating, WiFi, workspace. Study for hours with free refills. Polished but corporate. Seamless mobile ordering.

Dutch Bros = drive-thru-only (~900 sq ft stores). Broistas lean out the window, memorize your name, give you stickers. High-energy, personal. In and out in 2–3 minutes. 72% of transactions tied to the app.

The vibe test: Work/study → Starbucks. Quick + personal → Dutch Bros. Introvert/zero interaction → Starbucks mobile order. Extrovert/want help choosing → Dutch Bros broista.

The Bottom Line

Choose Starbucks: Sit and work, matcha options, maximum customization, richest rewards ecosystem.

Choose Dutch Bros: Fast drive-thru, energy drinks, non-coffee variety, personal broista experience, best welcome reward.

Choose both: Starbucks for morning study sessions, Dutch Bros for afternoon energy runs. Sipory recommends the best drink at whichever chain you're near. For the full three-chain comparison: Dunkin' vs Starbucks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dutch Bros cheaper than Starbucks?

For cold brew and energy drinks, yes. For signature espresso drinks, Dutch Bros is comparable or slightly more ($5.50–$6.49 vs $5.25). Dutch Bros' $1.75 Dutch Soda is the cheapest drink at any major chain.

Does Dutch Bros or Starbucks have more caffeine?

Starbucks wins on brewed coffee (Grande Blonde: 360mg). Dutch Bros' 9-1-1 (six shots, ~450mg) is one of the highest-caffeine chain drinks. Blue Rebel has ~152mg per small.

Which is better for non-coffee drinkers?

Dutch Bros — 40% of orders are non-coffee. Blue Rebels, Frost, Dutch Sodas, Smoothies, and Lemonades offer dozens of options.

Does Dutch Bros have a rewards program?

Yes — 3 pts/$1, free medium at 250 pts (~$83), free welcome drink of any size immediately, birthday drink valid 30 days.
