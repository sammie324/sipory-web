Healthiest Starbucks Drinks Under 150 Calories

March 20269 min read15 Picks · Calorie Table · Free Modifications

A Grande Caramel Frappuccino has 420 calories and 65g of sugar. A Grande Vanilla Latte with 2% milk has 250 calories. But a Grande Nitro Cold Brew has 5 calories and 0g sugar — and 280mg of caffeine. The healthiest Starbucks drinks aren't hidden. They're just not the ones promoted on the menu board.

This guide identifies the 15 best drinks under 150 calories, organizes them by category, and shows you free modifications that can cut 100+ calories from any order.

The pattern: the fewer ingredients, the fewer calories. Black coffee and Nitro are essentially zero-calorie. Once you add milk, the type matters enormously. Once you add syrup, pump count is the biggest calorie lever.

Build Your Own Healthy Drink

Step 1: Choose a base — Cold Brew (5 cal), Americano (10 cal), Brewed Coffee (5 cal), or Matcha (~40 cal powder).

Step 2: Choose milk — Almond (30 cal/serving) or nonfat (65 cal). A "splash" = about half the latte-portion calories.

Step 3: Choose sweetener — SF Vanilla or Caramel (0 cal), 1–2 pumps any syrup (20–40 cal), or Stevia (0 cal).

Step 4: Free topping — cinnamon, nutmeg, cocoa powder, or vanilla powder from the condiment bar (0 cal).

Example: Grande Cold Brew + splash almond + 1 pump SF vanilla + cinnamon = ~20 cal, 0g added sugar, 205mg caffeine, ~$4.25.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the healthiest drink at Starbucks?

Nitro Cold Brew: 5 cal, 0g sugar, 280mg caffeine. For flavor, an Iced Blonde Americano (10 cal) or Caffè Misto with almond milk (~50 cal).

How do I make my Starbucks order healthier?

Five free modifications: almond milk (saves ~90 cal), fewer pumps (20 cal each), skip whip (saves ~110 cal), Blonde Espresso, smaller size.

What Starbucks drinks have zero sugar?

Black brewed coffee, Americano, Espresso, Nitro, Cold Brew, and unsweetened teas. Sugar-Free Vanilla and Sugar-Free Caramel (new 2026) add flavor at 0g sugar.

How many calories are in a Starbucks latte?

Grande with 2%: ~190. Almond: ~100. Nonfat: ~130. Add 4 pumps flavored syrup for another ~80 cal.
