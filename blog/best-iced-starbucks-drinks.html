15 Best Iced Starbucks Drinks Ranked for 2026

March 202610 min readRanked by Category · Caffeine · Calories · Order Scripts

Cold drinks account for 75% of all Starbucks beverage sales — and that number climbs higher every year. The iced menu is massive: cold brews, iced coffees, shaken espressos, iced lattes, Refreshers, Frappuccinos, and iced teas. Some have 280mg of caffeine. Others have zero. Some are 5 calories. Others are 500.

This ranking covers the 15 best iced drinks across every category — with caffeine, calories, prices, and the exact words to say at the register. We included 2026 spring additions alongside year-round staples.

Top 5 In Detail

1Iced Brown Sugar Oatmilk Shaken Espresso Shaken Espresso
Grande · $5.75 · 255mg caffeine · ~120 cal
The fastest Starbucks drink to ever reach $1 billion in annual sales. Three Blonde Espresso shots shaken with brown sugar syrup and ice, topped with oat milk. The shaking creates micro-foam that gives it a frothy, almost latte-like texture without a full latte's calories. The brown sugar is warm and cookie-forward — like a brown butter cookie dissolved in espresso. This is what baristas drink on their breaks.
Say: "Can I get a grande iced brown sugar oatmilk shaken espresso?"
2Vanilla Sweet Cream Cold Brew Cold Brew
Grande · $5.25 · 205mg caffeine · ~200 cal
Cold Brew sweetened with vanilla syrup, topped with house-made vanilla sweet cream that cascades through the drink in beautiful swirls. The sweet cream is the star — rich, velvety, and slightly indulgent without being heavy. This drink converts people who think they don't like black coffee. The cold brew base is smooth and low-acid, and the sweet cream adds just enough dessert character. A year-round classic that never disappoints.
Say: "Can I get a grande vanilla sweet cream cold brew?"
3Nitro Cold Brew Cold Brew
Grande · $4.95 · 280mg caffeine · 5 cal
The healthiest high-caffeine drink on this list — 5 calories, zero sugar, and the nitrogen infusion creates a naturally creamy, stout-like texture. It tastes sweet without any sweetener. At 280mg, it's the second-strongest drink on the menu (behind Blonde Roast brewed coffee at 360mg). Only available in Tall and Grande, no ice allowed. If you want maximum caffeine with minimum calories, nothing beats it. See our healthy drinks guide for more zero-calorie options.
Say: "Can I get a grande Nitro Cold Brew?"
4Pink Drink Refresher
Grande · $5.45 · 45mg caffeine · ~140 cal
The most Instagrammed Starbucks drink of all time and a top-5 seller globally. Strawberry Açaí Refresher base shaken with coconut milk instead of water. Creamy, pink, and fruity with freeze-dried strawberries. It doesn't taste like coffee at all — it's a fruit-forward, lightly caffeinated tropical drink. The go-to recommendation for anyone who doesn't like coffee flavor. See our Refreshers guide for all variations.
Say: "Can I get a grande Pink Drink?"
5Iced Blonde Vanilla Latte Iced Latte
Grande · $5.25 · 170mg caffeine · ~190 cal
The best entry-point iced latte. Blonde Espresso is smoother and less bitter than the standard roast, which makes it more approachable — especially over ice where bitterness gets amplified. The vanilla adds sweetness without overwhelming. This is the drink to order if you're transitioning from non-coffee drinks to coffee drinks, or if you just want something reliably good. Swap to almond milk and sugar-free vanilla for a 60-calorie version.
Say: "Can I get a grande iced blonde vanilla latte?"

The Next 10

6Toasted Coconut Cream Cold Brew Cold Brew
Grande · $5.25 · 205mg caffeine · ~140 cal · Spring 2026 seasonal
The spring 2026 standout. Cold brew topped with toasted coconut cream cold foam — warm, tropical, and sophisticated. The "toasted" element adds a caramelized depth that prevents it from tasting like a coconut-flavored drink from a gas station. Limited time (likely through late April). For the full spring lineup, see our spring 2026 guide.
7Iced Matcha Latte Iced Latte
Grande · $5.25 · 80mg caffeine · ~200 cal
The non-coffee drink for people who want something earthy and sophisticated. Since the 2025 reformulation, the matcha is unsweetened by default — you control the sweetness. At 80mg caffeine with L-theanine for calm focus, matcha delivers a smoother, less jittery energy than coffee. Order with oat milk and 1 pump vanilla for the best balance. See our matcha guide.
8Dragon Drink Refresher
Grande · $5.45 · 45mg caffeine · ~150 cal
The Pink Drink's tropical counterpart — Mango Dragonfruit Refresher with coconut milk. Vivid magenta with dragonfruit pieces. More tropical and sweeter than the Pink Drink. The coconut milk creates a beautiful swirled effect. If the Pink Drink is a strawberry milkshake vibe, the Dragon Drink is a tropical smoothie vibe.
9Iced Lavender Cream Oatmilk Latte Iced Latte
Grande · ~$5.95 · ~170mg caffeine · ~180 cal · Spring 2026
Returning for its second spring season after a massive debut in 2024. Blonde Espresso with lavender syrup and oat milk, topped with lavender cream cold foam. Floral but not perfume-y — the espresso and oat milk ground it. Polarizing: floral-drink lovers adore it, while others find it soapy. Try a Tall before committing to a Grande.
10Iced Ube Coconut Macchiato Iced Latte
Grande · ~$5.75 · ~150mg caffeine · ~190 cal · Spring 2026 limited time
The trendiest drink on this list. Purple ube coconut cream layered under espresso shots with ube cold foam on top. Sweet, vanilla-like, and visually stunning. Limited time — get it before it disappears (likely late April). See our ube guide for the full cultural and flavor breakdown.
11Cold Brew (Black) Cold Brew
Grande · $4.25 · 205mg caffeine · 5 cal
The purist's pick. Steeped for 20 hours, naturally smooth, low acid, 5 calories. It's the cheapest cold brew option and gives you a blank canvas — add a splash of milk, a pump of syrup, or drink it straight. The best starting point for building a custom cold drink without overpaying. For the difference between iced coffee and cold brew, see our comparison guide.
12Iced Caramel Macchiato Iced Latte
Grande · $5.75 · 150mg caffeine · ~250 cal
The classic. Vanilla syrup, milk, espresso shots poured on top (they sink through), finished with caramel drizzle. The layered presentation is the appeal — espresso through vanilla milk with caramel sweetness. Higher calorie than most picks on this list, but the flavor-to-effort ratio is excellent. Ask for extra caramel drizzle (free) for maximum sweetness.
13Iced Chai Latte Iced Latte
Grande · $5.25 · 95mg caffeine · ~240 cal
The reimagined 2026 chai is a major upgrade — the chai concentrate and sweetener are now separate, so you control the sugar level. Default is fully sweetened; ask for "half sweet" or "unsweetened" to cut calories. The spice profile (cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, cloves) is bold over ice. Best non-coffee spiced drink on the menu.
14Strawberry Açaí Lemonade Refresher
Grande · $5.25 · 45mg caffeine · ~140 cal
The underrated Refresher. The lemonade version doesn't have a catchy name like "Pink Drink," but the tartness of lemonade balances the strawberry sweetness better than coconut milk or water. Tastes like a craft strawberry lemonade with a caffeine kick. Baristas often call this the best-tasting Refresher version. See our Refreshers ranking.
15Java Chip Frappuccino Frappuccino
Grande · $5.95 · ~105mg caffeine · ~440 cal
The only Frappuccino on this list — and it earns its spot as the best one. Mocha sauce, Frappuccino chips, milk, ice, blended and topped with whipped cream and mocha drizzle. It's essentially a chocolate chip milkshake with espresso. At 440 calories, this is a treat, not a daily driver. Order it "no whip" to save 110 calories, or try a Tall for a more reasonable portion.

How to Choose

Maximum caffeine: Nitro Cold Brew (280mg) or Iced Brown Sugar Oatmilk Shaken Espresso (255mg).

Lowest calories: Nitro Cold Brew (5 cal) or Black Cold Brew (5 cal). See our full health guide.

No coffee flavor: Pink Drink, Dragon Drink, or Iced Matcha Latte. See our non-coffee guide.

Best value: Iced Coffee at ~$3.45 is the cheapest. Black Cold Brew at ~$4.25 is the best caffeine-per-dollar ratio among premium drinks. See our budget guide.

Best for customization: Black Cold Brew — add any syrup, milk, or cold foam to build your perfect drink. See our customization guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best iced drink at Starbucks?

The Iced Brown Sugar Oatmilk Shaken Espresso — the fastest drink to reach $1 billion in Starbucks sales. High caffeine (255mg), balanced sweetness, frothy shaken texture. For non-coffee drinkers, the Pink Drink is the top pick.

What is the strongest iced Starbucks drink?

Nitro Cold Brew at 280mg per Grande. Among popular iced options, the Shaken Espresso (255mg) and Cold Brew (205mg) are next. Refreshers are the weakest at 45mg.

What is the cheapest iced drink at Starbucks?

Iced Coffee at ~$3.45 for a Grande with 165mg caffeine. Cold Brew is ~$4.25. Best caffeine-per-dollar: Iced Coffee at ~48mg per dollar.

What iced Starbucks drinks have no coffee?

Refreshers (all flavors), Iced Passion Tango Tea, Iced Matcha Latte, Iced Chai Latte, Blended Lemonades, and Vanilla Bean Frappuccino. Refreshers and matcha still contain caffeine from non-coffee sources.
