Starbucks Summer Drinks 2026: What's Coming and What to Order

March 20269 min readUpdated as Launches Drop · Timeline · Best Iced Picks

Summer is Starbucks' longest seasonal window — roughly 14 weeks of iced-drink dominance across two menu waves. Cold drinks already account for 75% of Starbucks beverage sales, and that number climbs above 85% during summer months. The summer 2026 menu hasn't been fully announced yet, but between confirmed April 7 launches, Starbucks Latin America leaks, and historical patterns, we can map out what's coming month by month.

This article is updated as new launches are confirmed. Last updated: March 25, 2026.

What We Can Predict (and Why)

Starbucks' summer strategy has been remarkably consistent for the past three years. Here are the patterns that inform our predictions:

Popping pearls are almost certainly returning. The raspberry popping pearls that debuted with the Summer-Berry Refresher in 2025 were Starbucks' most talked-about summer innovation in years. They drove measurable increases in summer traffic and social media engagement. Starbucks has invested in the supply chain infrastructure for pearls — it would be unusual to not leverage that investment again. Expect either the Summer-Berry Refresher to return or a new pearl-based drink to replace it.

At least one new Frappuccino. Despite the broader menu simplification under Brian Niccol, Frappuccinos are summer's highest-demand category. Starbucks launched new Frappuccino variants every summer from 2019–2025. The "Strato" platform (layered Frappuccinos with cold foam tops) that appeared in Latin America markets could be the 2026 US version.

Tropical and fruit-forward flavors dominate. Summer menus skew bright, fruity, and cold. Mango has been a recurring theme (Mango Dragonfruit Refresher, Mango Cream Matcha). Passion fruit, guava, and watermelon are all possible. The April 7 Mango Cream launches signal mango as 2026's fruit-of-the-year.

Matcha continues expanding. With 5+ matcha drinks already year-round and the Mango Cream Matcha launching in April, expect at least one summer-exclusive matcha variation — possibly a tropical matcha Frappuccino or a matcha-fruit Refresher hybrid.

The Best Iced Drinks to Order Right Now

While waiting for summer, these are the top picks from the current menu — all optimized for warm weather:

Toasted Coconut Cream Cold Brew Available Now
Grande · ~$5.25 · 205mg caffeine · Spring 2026 seasonal
The most summer-appropriate spring drink. Cold brew topped with toasted coconut cream cold foam — warm, tropical, and refreshing without being heavy. The coconut flavor is subtle (toasted, not piña colada). This is the best current-menu drink for summer vibes ahead of the actual summer launch.
Say: "Can I get a grande Toasted Coconut Cream Cold Brew?"
Dragon Drink (Mango Dragonfruit Refresher + Coconut Milk) Year-Round
Grande · ~$5.45 · 45mg caffeine · Permanent menu
The quintessential summer Starbucks drink — vibrant magenta, tropical mango, creamy coconut milk, dragonfruit inclusions. Available year-round but it tastes best when it's 90 degrees outside. Low caffeine makes it a good afternoon option. For the full Refresher breakdown, see our Refreshers guide.
Say: "Can I get a grande Dragon Drink?"
Iced Brown Sugar Oatmilk Shaken Espresso Year-Round
Grande · ~$5.75 · 255mg caffeine · Permanent menu
The #1 barista-recommended drink regardless of season, but especially good iced in summer. Three shots of Blonde Espresso shaken with brown sugar and oat milk — cookie-forward flavor, high caffeine, and the shaking creates an almost frothy iced texture. This is Starbucks' fastest drink to ever reach $1 billion in sales.
Say: "Can I get a grande iced brown sugar oatmilk shaken espresso?"
Vanilla Sweet Cream Cold Brew Year-Round
Grande · ~$5.25 · 205mg caffeine · Permanent menu
The cold brew + sweet cream layering is a summer classic. Vanilla syrup in the cold brew, house-made vanilla sweet cream floating on top. Smooth, sweet, and visually satisfying as the cream cascades down. A reliable choice when you want something cold and caffeinated without being adventurous.
Say: "Can I get a grande vanilla sweet cream cold brew?"
Nitro Cold Brew Year-Round
Grande · ~$4.95 · 280mg caffeine · 5 calories · Permanent menu
The healthiest high-caffeine summer option: 5 calories, zero sugar, naturally creamy from the nitrogen infusion. It's served without ice (ice disrupts the nitrogen cascade), but the cold brew base is already cold. At 280mg, it's one of the strongest drinks on the menu. For the full health angle, see our healthiest drinks guide.
Say: "Can I get a grande Nitro Cold Brew?"
