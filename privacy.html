Privacy Policy

We value transparency and respect your privacy. Here is a clear breakdown of how Sipory handles your data.

Last Updated: February 2026

Data Collection

We only collect what is necessary to provide you with a personalized coffee experience. This includes your taste preferences, saved drinks, and order history. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Usage & Analytics

To improve Sipory, we use anonymous usage data. This helps us understand which features are most valuable and how we can better serve your coffee needs.

Important Note
You can opt out of anonymous analytics tracking at any time in your Settings.

Your Rights

You retain full ownership of your data. You may request a copy of your information or ask for account deletion by contacting support. We will process your request within 30 days.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for authentication and data storage, providing enterprise-grade security for your information.

Third-Party Services

Sipory integrates with the following third-party services to deliver its functionality: Supabase (authentication and data storage), RevenueCat (subscription management), and anonymous analytics. Each service processes data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Sipory is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Sipory after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
